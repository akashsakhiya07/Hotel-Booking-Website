Frequently Asked Questions

Booking & Reservations

How do I make a reservation?

You can make a reservation through our website, mobile app, or by calling our reservation desk. Simply select your dates, choose a room type, and follow the booking process.

What is your cancellation policy?

We offer free cancellation up to 24 hours before check-in. For late cancellations, a fee may apply. Please refer to our terms and conditions for detailed information.

Can I modify my booking?

Yes, you can modify your booking through your account dashboard or by contacting our customer service team. Changes are subject to availability.

Check-in & Check-out

What are your check-in and check-out times?

Check-in time is 3:00 PM and check-out time is 12:00 PM. Early check-in and late check-out are subject to availability and may incur additional charges.

Do you offer early check-in?

Early check-in is available on a first-come, first-served basis. Please contact us in advance to request early check-in.

Amenities & Services

What amenities are included in the room?

All rooms include free Wi-Fi, air conditioning, flat-screen TV, mini-bar, coffee maker, and complimentary toiletries. Additional amenities vary by room type.

Is parking available?

Yes, we offer complimentary parking for all guests. Valet parking service is also available for an additional fee.

Payment & Pricing

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, and online payment methods. Cash payments are also accepted at the hotel.

Do you offer any special rates?

Yes, we offer special rates for corporate bookings, group reservations, and long-term stays. Please contact our sales team for more information.
